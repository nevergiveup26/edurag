## Unit 1: How can I get there?

|:-----|:-----|:-----|:-----|:---------|:-----|

| museum | /mju:'zi:əm/ | n. | 博物馆 | art museum 美术馆 | I want to go to the museum. |
| post office | /pəʊst 'ɒfɪs/ | n. | 邮局 | go to the post office | Where is the post office? |
| bookstore | /'bʊkstɔ:(r)/ | n. | 书店 | in the bookstore | Let's go to the bookstore. |

| crossing | /'krɒsɪŋ/ | n. | 十字路口 | at the crossing | Turn right at the crossing. |
| turn | /tɜ:n/ | v. | 转弯 | turn left/right | Turn left at the bookstore. |
| left | /left/ | adj./adv. | 左边 | on the left / turn left | The museum is on your left. |
| right | /raɪt/ | adj./adv. | 右边 | on the right / turn right | Turn right, then go straight. |
| straight | /streɪt/ | adv. | 笔直地 | go straight | Go straight for five minutes. |
| far | /fɑ:(r)/ | adj. | 远的 | far from | It's not far from here. |

| interesting | /'ɪntrəstɪŋ/ | adj. | 有趣的 | an interesting place | It's an interesting film. |

### 重点句型

1. **问路**：Where is the ... ?（……在哪里？）
2. **指路**：It's near/next to ... / Turn left/right at ...
3. **表达远近距离**：It's far from here. / It's not far.
