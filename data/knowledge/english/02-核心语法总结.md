## 一、一般现在时（Simple Present Tense）

### 定义与用法
表示经常性、习惯性的动作或状态，以及客观事实和真理。

### 结构

| 句式 | 含 be 动词 | 含行为动词（非三单） | 含行为动词（三单） |
|:-----|:-----------|:-------------------|:-------------------|
| 肯定句 | I am a student. | I play football. | He **plays** football. |
| 否定句 | I am **not** a student. | I **don't** play football. | He **doesn't** play football. |
| 一般疑问句 | **Are** you a student? | **Do** you play football? | **Does** he play football? |
| 特殊疑问句 | What are you? | What do you play? | What does he play? |

### 动词第三人称单数变化规则

| 规则 | 原形 | 三单 | 记忆口诀 |
|:-----|:-----|:-----|:---------|
| 一般情况 +s | play | plays | 一般加 s |
| 以 s/x/sh/ch/o 结尾 +es | watch, go | watches, goes | 嘶嘶声加 es |
| 辅音+y → 变 y 为 i+es | fly, study | flies, studies | y 变 ies |
| 元音+y → 直接 +s | play, say | plays, says | 元音y不变 |
| 不规则 | have | has | 特殊要记牢 |

### 时间标志词
always, usually, often, sometimes, never, every day/week/month/year, on Mondays

### 例句
- **描述习惯**：I **get up** at seven every morning.
- **描述事实**：The earth **goes** around the sun.
- **描述状态**：She **is** a teacher.
- **三单否定**：He **doesn't like** milk.

### 常见错误
❌ He **play** football. → ✓ He **plays** football.（三单忘加 s）
❌ She **don't** like music. → ✓ She **doesn't** like music.（三单否定用 doesn't，动词还原）
❌ **Does** he **plays** football? → ✓ **Does** he **play** football?（does 后动词还原）

## 二、一般现在时与现在进行时的区别

### 对比表

| 对比项 | 一般现在时 | 现在进行时 |
|:-------|:-----------|:-----------|
| 用法 | 经常性、习惯性动作 | 此刻正在进行的动作 |
| 结构 | 动词原形/三单 | am/is/are + V-ing |
| 标志词 | always, usually, every day | now, look, listen, at the moment |
| 例句 | I **read** books every day. | I **am reading** a book now. |

### 常见错误
❌ I **am reading** every day. → ✓ I **read** every day.（每天 → 一般现在时）
❌ Look! He **plays** basketball. → ✓ Look! He **is playing** basketball.（看！→ 现在进行时）

## 三、一般过去时（Simple Past Tense）

### 定义与用法
表示过去某个时间发生的动作或存在的状态。

### 结构

| 句式 | 含 be 动词 | 含行为动词 |
|:-----|:-----------|:-----------|
| 肯定句 | I **was** happy yesterday. | I **played** football yesterday. |
| 否定句 | I **wasn't** happy. | I **didn't play** football. |
| 一般疑问句 | **Were** you happy? | **Did** you play football? |
| 特殊疑问句 | Where **were** you? | What **did** you play? |

### 动词过去式变化规则

**规则变化：**

| 规则 | 原形 | 过去式 | 口诀 |
|:-----|:-----|:-------|:-----|
| 一般情况 +ed | play, work | played, worked | 一般加 ed |
| 以不发音 e 结尾 +d | like, live | liked, lived | e结尾加 d |
| 辅音+y → ied | study, cry | studied, cried | y 变 ied |
| 重读闭音节双写 +ed | stop, plan | stopped, planned | 重闭双写加 ed |

**常见不规则变化：**

| 原形 | 过去式 | 原形 | 过去式 |
|:-----|:-------|:-----|:-------|
| am/is | was | go | went |
| are | were | see | saw |
| do/does | did | come | came |
| have/has | had | get | got |
| take | took | buy | bought |
| eat | ate | swim | swam |
| read | read（读/rɛd/） | run | ran |
| make | made | sing | sang |

### 时间标志词
yesterday, last night/week/month/year, ... ago, in 2020, just now

### 例句
- I **was** at home yesterday.
- We **went** to the zoo last Sunday.
- She **didn't do** her homework last night.
- **Did** you **see** the film? — Yes, I did. / No, I didn't.

### 常见错误
❌ He **didn't went** to school. → ✓ He **didn't go** to school.（did 后动词还原）
❌ I **see** him yesterday. → ✓ I **saw** him yesterday.（过去时间用过去式）
❌ **Did** he **watched** TV? → ✓ **Did** he **watch** TV?（did 后动词还原）

## 四、一般将来时（Simple Future Tense）

### 定义与用法
表示将来某个时间要发生的动作或存在的状态。

### be going to 结构

| 句式 | 结构 | 例句 |
|:-----|:-----|:-----|
| 肯定句 | 主语 + am/is/are + going to + 动词原形 | I **am going to visit** my grandparents. |
| 否定句 | 主语 + am/is/are + **not** + going to + 动词原形 | He **isn't going to swim**. |
| 一般疑问句 | Am/Is/Are + 主语 + going to + 动词原形? | **Are** you going to see a film? |
| 特殊疑问句 | 疑问词 + am/is/are + 主语 + going to + 动词原形? | **What are** you going to do? |

### will 结构（拓展）

| 句式 | 例句 |
|:-----|:-----|
| 肯定句 | I **will go** to Beijing. |
| 否定句 | I **won't go** to Beijing. |
| 疑问句 | **Will** you go to Beijing? |

### 时间标志词
tomorrow, next week/month/year, tonight, this evening, soon, in the future

### 例句
- What **are** you **going to do** this weekend?
- We **are going to take** a trip next month.
- She **isn't going to buy** a dictionary.
- **Where are** you going?

### 常见错误
❌ I **am going to** the park. → 这表示"正在去"，不是将来。将来应为：I'm going to **go to** the park.
❌ He **going to visit** me. → ✓ He **is going to visit** me.（少了 be 动词）
❌ She **will goes** to school. → ✓ She **will go** to school.（will 后动词还原）

## 五、现在进行时（Present Continuous Tense）

### 定义与用法
表示现在（说话时）正在进行或发生的动作。

### 结构

| 句式 | 结构 | 例句 |
|:-----|:-----|:-----|
| 肯定句 | 主语 + am/is/are + V-ing | I **am reading**. |
| 否定句 | 主语 + am/is/are + not + V-ing | He **isn't sleeping**. |
| 一般疑问句 | Am/Is/Are + 主语 + V-ing? | **Are** you listening? |
| 特殊疑问句 | 疑问词 + am/is/are + 主语 + V-ing? | What **are** you doing? |

### V-ing 变化规则

| 规则 | 原形 | V-ing | 口诀 |
|:-----|:-----|:------|:-----|
| 一般情况 +ing | play, read | playing, reading | 一般加 ing |
| 不发音 e 结尾去 e+ing | make, write | making, writing | 去 e 加 ing |
| 重读闭音节双写 +ing | run, swim, sit | running, swimming, sitting | 重闭双写加 ing |
| ie 结尾变 y+ing | die, lie | dying, lying | ie 变 y 加 ing |

### 时间标志词
now, look!, listen!, at the moment, right now, these days

### 例句
- Look! The boy **is running**.
- Listen! She **is singing**.
- What **are** you **doing** now? — I **am doing** my homework.

### 常见错误
❌ He **swiming** in the pool. → ✓ He **is swimming** in the pool.（少了 be 动词；swim 要双写 m）
❌ I **am geting** up. → ✓ I **am getting** up.（get 重读闭音节，双写 t）
❌ She **making** a cake. → ✓ She **is making** a cake.（少了 be 动词）

## 六、形容词比较级（Comparative Adjectives）

### 定义与用法
比较两个人或事物的差别，"比……更……"。

### 结构
```
A + be + 比较级 + than + B
```

### 比较级变化规则

| 规则 | 原级 | 比较级 | 口诀 |
|:-----|:-----|:-------|:-----|
| 单音节 +er | tall, short | taller, shorter | 一般加 er |
| 以 e 结尾 +r | large, nice | larger, nicer | e结尾加 r |
| 重读闭音节双写 +er | big, hot, fat | bigger, hotter, fatter | 重闭双写加 er |
| 辅音+y → ier | heavy, happy | heavier, happier | y 变 ier |
| 多音节 more + 原级 | beautiful, interesting | more beautiful, more interesting | 多音加 more |

### 不规则变化

| 原级 | 比较级 |
|:-----|:-------|
| good/well | better |
| bad/badly | worse |
| many/much | more |
| little | less |
| far | farther/further |

### 例句
- I am **taller than** you.
- This book is **more interesting than** that one.
- He is **better at** English than me.
- Shanghai is **bigger than** Beijing.

### 常见错误
❌ He is **taller then** me. → ✓ He is **taller than** me.（是 than 不是 then）
❌ She is **more tall** than me. → ✓ She is **taller** than me.（tall 是单音节，+er 不 +more）
❌ This is **more better**. → ✓ This is **better**.（better 本身是比较级，不需 more）

## 七、重要句型与交际用语

### 问路指路
- Where is the museum? — It's near the hospital.
- How can I get there? — Turn left at the bookstore, then go straight.

### 出行方式
- How do you come to school? — I usually come on foot. / By bus.
- I must pay attention to the traffic lights.

### 周末计划（一般将来时）
- What are you going to do this weekend? — I'm going to see a film.
- Where are you going? — I'm going to the supermarket.

### 谈论兴趣爱好
- What are your hobbies? — I like singing and dancing.
- He likes doing kung fu and swimming.
- Does she like reading stories? — Yes, she does.

### 谈论职业
- What does your father do? — He is a businessman.
- Where does he work? — He works in a factory.
- How does he go to work? — He goes to work by car.

### 谈论感受与建议
- How do you feel? — I feel sad.
- What's wrong? — My pet dog is ill.
- Don't be sad. You should see a doctor.
